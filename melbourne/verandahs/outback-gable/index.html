Clearspan Gable

The Outback Clearspan Gable uses only the Outback Deck, a very strong material that does not need extra purlins to support it. This leaves the roof area clean and uncluttered. The Clearspan Gable can span an impressive 7.5 metres wide with a 22 degree pitch.
